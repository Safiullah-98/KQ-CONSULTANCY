About Us

KQ Engineering Consultancy LLC stands as a premier integrated provider of engineering consultancy solutions, renowned for excellence in the field. With a strong foothold in the engineering consultancy services sector, our expertise sets us apart.

We possess an in-depth familiarity with the current International Building Code (IBC) requisites, in addition to our proficiency in earlier iterations and model building codes. Our team of engineers is actively engaged in their respective domains, upholding affiliations with esteemed professional and technical societies. Our commitment lies in furnishing our clientele with ingenious, secure, economically viable, and expeditious solutions to their structural quandaries.

The collective force of KQ Engineers and Consultants is dedicated to delivering tangible, value-enhancing outcomes to infrastructure and urban development undertakings across the Middle East.
